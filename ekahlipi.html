Preface

Ekahlipi is not a collection of scripts, but a quiet convergence of silence, language, and introspection. These writings invite the reader to pause, observe, and experience unity beyond structured thought.

About the Book

This book explores language as presence rather than ornament. Through reflective scripts, it journeys across solitude, emotional realism, and the subtle bonds that connect human experience into oneness.
