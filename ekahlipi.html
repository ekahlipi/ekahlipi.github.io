Preface

Ekahlipi is not a collection of scripts but a convergence of silence, language, and philosophical inquiry. These writings explore unity, fragmentation, and the unseen bonds that hold human experience together.

About the Book

This book examines language not as a tool, but as a living entity. Each script moves through themes of solitude, emotional realism, cultural memory, and oneness — without spectacle, without force.
